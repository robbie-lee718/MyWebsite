About me

My Education

I am a current Senior at Auburn University majoring in Computer Science with a certificate in Artificial Intelligence. At Auburn I have been in the Auburn University Marching Band for 3 years and am a member of the National Society of Collegiate Scholars. While in the Marching Band I participated in light crew for both the "Vive la France" and "Game On" shows. This included assembling, testing, fixing, and distributing lighting rigs for these shows.

My Experience

I have interned at OCV for over a year. OCV is one of the leaders in mobile app development for law enforcement, public safety, emergency management and public health agencies for over ten years.

My hobbies

I enjoy creating small projects in my free time, such as video games and apps. I also enjoy playing and listening to music of all varieties. In my free time I like to play disc golf, work on projects, play video games, and go to the gym. Ultimately, I love learning how things work and creating things based on my knowledge.
